Chargeback Management

Chargebacks are constantly becoming a major threat to online business, so don’t let chargebacks affect your business bottom line. As a business owner, you might be probably thinking one chargeback doesn’t sound too bad. However, it can quickly add up and impact the bottom line of any growing business and lead to closure of merchant accounts.

What is a chargeback?

Chargeback in simpler terms is a dispute raised by a card holder (via their issuing bank) against the merchant, to initiate a refund for a purchase made on their card. In the chargeback process, the burden of proof lies with the merchant. The merchant will be given an opportunity to provide supporting documentation to prove the legitimacy of the transaction. If the merchant is successful, the transaction is credited back to his account. If the merchant is unsuccessful, or does not respond in a timely fashion, they will be financially responsible for returning funds to the consumer who filed the dispute. Reasons for Chargeback There are many reasons why a cardholder may dispute the transaction and cause a chargeback to your merchant account.

  • Fraudulent transactions

  • Credit Not Processed

    • Cancelled Recurring

  • Duplicate Processing

  • Service Not Provided / Merchandise Not Received

  • Transaction Not Recognized

Chargeback Prevention Management

Why an Alerts services?

Any Merchant Account faces a risk of getting closed when the chargeback ratio is more than the bank’s set threshold, which in turn will impact the business sales and growth and would also never be able to get another merchant account under the same corp. Chargeback Alerts are key to stop the chargeback before it hits the merchant account.

How will Chargeback Alerts help merchants prevent chargebacks?

A Chargeback Alert is a notification from a network of banks in real time letting you know that a potential fraud transaction has been confirmed by the issuing bank or a customer has initiated a dispute. This real time notification will give you the opportunity to accept and refund the alert there by will preventing the chargeback from hitting your account.

When you are a merchant, you need chargeback alerts and a chargeback management program that will help you take immediate steps to stop chargeback through alerts, prevent chargebacks and avoid chargeback fees.

Why Chargeback Patrols?

We help preventing the chargeback for both fraud and non-fraud chargebacks. Patrols have a dedicated team working 24/7 to process the refund for the alert. To avoid chargebacks, refunds must be processed within 24 hours of receiving the alert – this includes alerts received on the weekends and holidays. If an alerts are not processed within the 24 hour window they turn in to chargebacks and in turn increase the risk level leading to closure of the merchant account.
